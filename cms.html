Content Management Services

What is CMS?

Content Management System (CMS) is a software or a suite of applications deployed to input content on a website seamlessly. It is an interface or a medium for the creation, edition or modification, review and publishing of material on a site and enabling multiple users to upload content at the same time and work collaboratively on the website.

CMS offers a web-based graphic user interface (GUI) enabling publishers to access CMS through a web browser. CMS designed for web publishing provides options and features such as the indexing and search of documents and specifying keywords and metadata for search engine crawlers, etc. At the same time, the CMS abstracts away the low-level details unless they are required.

Know in Depth !!

Essentially CMS is an interface for the management of the content of a website. CMS has two components: Content Management Application and Content Delivery Application. Content Management Application is a front-end user interface used to add, modify, remove content from a website even with limited expertise and without any knowledge of HTML. Content Delivery Application compiles that information and updates the site.

We architect the Content Management System for our clients depending on the publication requirements.

  • Online help
  • Group and user functionality
  • Support for changing template designs
  • Installation of various wizards and their up-gradation
  • Audit logs & all other features as required by the client.
